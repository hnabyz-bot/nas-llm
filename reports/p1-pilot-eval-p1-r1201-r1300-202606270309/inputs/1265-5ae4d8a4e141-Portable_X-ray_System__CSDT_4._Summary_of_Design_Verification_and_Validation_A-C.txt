Determine the PMCF needs
The objective of the post market clinical follow-up(PMCF) is to confirm performance and safety
throughout the expected lifetime of the medical device, the acceptability of identified risks and
to detect emerging risks on the basis of factual evidence.
The decision to conduct PMCF study was based on the identification of possible residual risks
and unclarity on long term clinical performance that may impact the benefit-risk ratio.
Conclusion of PMCF needs
The PMCF study is not required for the HnX-P1, HnX-PB because the safety and clinical
performance are already known from previous use of the device. And the appropriate post-
market surveillance can provide sufficient data to address the risks.
