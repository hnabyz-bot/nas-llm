The selection/criteria

The literatures were analyzed considering the inclusion and exclusion criteria. At first, the application of selection criteria was limited to the literatures’ metadata (title, abstract, and keywords). The metadata of each literature were read by the searcher(s), who also considered the application of the inclusion and exclusion criteria. In addition, the selection criteria had regard to the content of the introduction and conclusion of the literatures.

Potentially relevant literature identified through the search

64 literatures of which titles and abstracts were screened for relevance

Literature retrieved for more detailed assessment

8 literatures used for this clinical evaluation report.

The 48 literatures are excluded, for this reason:

- The full text is not available.

The 8 literatures are excluded for this reason:

- There is a newer or a more complete study about the same research.

- The literature is not related to Flat Panel Digital X-ray detector.

- Flat Panel Digital X-ray detector is not the main focus of the literature.

First Criterion:

Full Text Written in English

Second Criterion:

Relevance

If any data is searched in double, it must be analyzed to select proper data.

If the searched data show the same results in performance and safety, even only such a few data can be reviewed as clinical proof and used usefully.

If the searched data show different results from each other in performance and safety, deeper analysis is needed and such analysis results must be written in Clinical Evaluation Report.

If the searched data is determined to be used, it must be documented in Literature Search Report.

Data appraisal and weighting must have complied with Section 9, MEDDEV 2.7/1 (Rev. 4, 2016)

Appraisal of collected data

Methodology

Inclusion Criteria

The literatures addressing any of the topics listed below were included:

The literature is generated from the actual use of the equivalent devices (DRX-1, CXDI-31, CXDI-70C, CXDI-11 and etc.).

The literature is related to Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.

The literature addresses the usefulness of the Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.

The literature addresses the safety of Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.

The literature addresses the performance of Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.

Exclusion Criteria

The literatures addressing any of the exclusion criteria listed below were excluded:

The literature is not related to Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.

The literature doesn’t address the safety and performance of the Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.

The Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector is not the main focus of the literature.

There is a newer or a more complete study about the same research.

The full text is not available.

Criteria for selection

For the literature, the weighting is assigned by grading. So, the category is as follows;

17~22 point: Sufficient for scientific Literature databases

13~16 point: Not enough for scientific Literature databases, but available for clinical databases

9~12 point: Inappropriate for clinical evaluation

Abstract

Purpose:

To assess the impact of additional Cu filtration with the DRX-1 detector in optimisation of adult chest radiography.

Methods:

A simulated anatomical phantom was imaged at different levels of Cu filtration (0 mm, 0.1 mm, 0.3 mm and 0.5 mm of Cu) at 120 kVp. Conclusion:
