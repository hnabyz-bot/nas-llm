Biocompatibility

The ADD contains parts which is intended to be contacted with the body of patient.

So, we perform the systemic approach to a biological evaluation of medical devices according to the ISO 10993-1. The ADD has direct contact component (Intact skin, under 24 hours). Material of the ADD is same as in commercially available device. But it does not have the same chemical composition with the predicate device.
