General Information

 Introduction

This risk analysis is performed on “HAD1717MC/HAD1717MG”, we design and manufacture for the purpose of investigating the risk of this device. This analysis was planned at the beginning stage of device design, and report is prepared at the stage of initial lot production. In line with the change of risk and technology on the device this analysis will be performed together. Main methods and procedures we selected herewith are focused on EN ISO 14971:2019 and EN 60601-1:2006/A1:2013.

 Device information

Product Name: Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector

Model Name: HAD1717MC, HAD1717MG

 Intended use

HAD1717MC(MG),Digital Flat Panel X-Ray Detector is indicated for digital imaging solution designed for providing general radiographic diagnosis of human anatomy targeting both adult and children. It is intended to replace film based radiographic diagnostic systems and provide more precise case diagnosis for physicians and radiologists. Not to be used for mammography.

 Overview

This is a wired digital flat panel x-ray detector, a faster and more efficient digital radiography system. The detector is used in combination with a TFT glass and scintillator (GDX or CSI) and supports automatic trigger signal detection technology that can be used without generator integration.

The incident X-rays are converted into visible light that produces electron hole pairs in a photometer biased by scintillator material. The charge carrier is stored in the photodiode capacity. By pulse processing the gates of the TFT lines in the matrix, charges in all the columns are transferred in parallel with the signal output. All signals on the column are amplified by a custom Read Out IC for further processing.

The amplified signal is digitized using an A / D converter integrated into the ROIC. The digitized signal is transmitted to the PC.
