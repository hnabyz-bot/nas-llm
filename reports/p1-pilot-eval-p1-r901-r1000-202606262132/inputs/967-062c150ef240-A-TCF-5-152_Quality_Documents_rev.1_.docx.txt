Description of Quality Control

Quality manual

Our Quality Manual(AQM-001) is designed to provide medical devices and related services that meet legal requirements (ISO 13485:2016, KGMP (Korea Good Manufacturing Practices), FDA CFR Part 820-QSR:2017, EU 2017/745) and customer requirements. The purpose is to establish a quality management system that stipulates our company's organizational operation and management, responsibilities and authorities.

Working environment and infrastructure control

Management of the working environment is carried out in accordance with the working environment, safety and hygiene management procedure(AQP605), and by determining matters related to the safety management of our employees and the cleaning and disinfection methods of the workplace and equipment necessary for product production, and worker's hygiene. Its purpose is to thoroughly manage environmental hygiene, secure quality, prevent contamination, and protect workers from the surrounding environment.

In addition, manufacturing facility management is conducted according to the manufacturing facility management procedure(AQP603), and the purpose is to contribute to smooth production activities by continuously demonstrating the capability of the facility.

Incoming control

The purchase of raw materials is carried out according to the purchase management procedure, and related suppliers are managed according to the supplier management procedure.

In addition, incoming raw materials are inspected according to the inspection and testing procedures, and nonconforming products are handled according to the nonconforming product management procedure(AQP802).

In process control

Process control is conducted in accordance with process control procedure(AQP707) and aims to identify and plan manufacturing processes, including personnel, facilities, equipment, techniques and methods that directly affect product quality, and to ensure that these processes are performed under controlled conditions. there is In addition, process validation procedure(AQP711) are used to validate whether the process, facility, or facility functions as intended.

Equipment control

Instruments used for product inspection are managed according to instrument management procedure(AQP709), and the purpose is to contribute to product quality assurance and quality improvement by maintaining precision and accuracy and making the operating system efficient.
