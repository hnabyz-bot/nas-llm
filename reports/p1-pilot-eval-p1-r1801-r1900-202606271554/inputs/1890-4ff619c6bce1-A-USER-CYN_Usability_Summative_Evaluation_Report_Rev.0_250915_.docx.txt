Overview 개요

This document is a comprehensive evaluation report of H&ABIZ Co., Ltd. for the usability engineering process of medical devices. The purpose of this comprehensive evaluation report is to confirm and evaluate whether the defined risks and usage errors of the Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector (Ref no.: GT1717C, GT1717CP) have been appropriately controlled through the preceding usability engineering process, and to make a final evaluation of whether the representative user can interact with the given device effectively and safely. 본 문서는 의료기기의 사용적합성 엔지니어링 프로세스를 위한 ㈜에이치앤아비즈 의 총괄 평가 보고서이다. 본 총괄 평가 보고서는 앞선 사용적합성 엔지니어링 프로세스를 통해 Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector(Model no. GT1717C, GT1717CP)의 정의된 위험과 사용오류가 적절히 통제되었는지 확인하고 평가하여 대표 사용자가 주어진 기기와 효과적이고 안전하게 상호작용할 수 있는지 최종 평가하는 것을 목적으로 한다.

Intended Use

의도된 사용 목적

The HnX-R1(Digital X-ray Imaging System) is indicated for digital imaging solution designed for providing general radiographic diagnosis of human anatomy targeting both adult and children. It is intended to replace film based radiographic diagnostic system. Not to be used for mammography.

How it works

작동 원리

This is a wired digital flat panel x-ray detector, a faster and more efficient digital radiography system. The detector is used in combination with a TFT glass and scintillator and supports automatic trigger signal detection technology that can be used without generator integration.

 The incident X-rays are converted into visible light that produces electron hole pairs in a photometer biased by scintillator material. The charge carrier is stored in the photodiode capacity. By pulse processing the gates of the TFT lines in the matrix, charges in all the columns are transferred in parallel with the signal output. All signals on the column are amplified by a custom Read Out IC for further processing.

 The amplified signal is digitized using an A/D converter integrated into the ROIC. The digitized signal is transmitted to the PC.
