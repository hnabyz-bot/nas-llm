Basis

- Estimated life of KMG Series capacitor from Samyoung Electronics.

http://www.samyoung.co.kr/admincenter/files/prod/KMG.pdf

- Capacitor estimated life calculation

L = L0 x 2^((Tmax - Ta)/10)

L: Estimated life (hour)

L0: Life at rated temperature (hour) = 2,000 Hours

Tmax: Rated Temperature (℃) = 105℃

Ta: Ambient Temperature (℃) = 59℃

L = 2000 x 2^((105 – 59)/10)

- Result (suppose): 48,502.9 Hours@59℃

Frequency of use

- Average daily use

: Less than 9 hours per day

- Reasonably foreseeable worst usage

: Using for 24 hours a day with a battery charger plugged in

1 day = 24 hours

1 week = 168 hours 1 week = 7 days

1 month = 720 hours 1 month = 30 days

1 year = 8,760 hours 1 year = 365 days

ESL: About 5 years
