0. Introduction
· General
Manufacturers of class IIa, class IIb, and class III devices shall prepare a periodic safety update
report in accordance with the article 86 of the Regulation (EU) 2017/745 (MDR).
This is a report for the Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector to summarise results and conclusions of the
analyses of the post-market surveillance data gathered as a result of the post-market surveillance
plan referred to the Regulation (EU) 2017/745 together with a rationale and description of any
preventive and corrective actions taken.
· Related references
- Regulation (EU) 2017/745 of the European Parliament and of the Council
- Post-market clinical follow-up files
- Risk management files
- Clinical evaluation files
· PSUR submission system for each class
For class III devices or implantable devices, manufacturers shall submit PSURs by means of the
electronic system referred to in Article 92 of MDR to the notified body involved in the conformity
assessment in accordance with Article 52. The notified body shall review the report and add its
evaluation to that electronic system with details of any action taken. Such PSURs and the evaluation
by the notified body shall be made available to competent authorities through that electronic system.
For devices other than those, manufacturers shall make PSURs available to the notified body
involved in the conformity assessment and, upon request, to competent authorities.

(1) Inclusion Criteria
The literatures addressing any of the topics listed below were included:
- The literature is generated from the actual use of the equivalent devices
(DRX-1, CXDI – 31, CXDI-70C and etc.).
- The literature is related to Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.
- The literature addresses the usefulness of the Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.
- The literature addresses the safety of Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.
- The literature addresses the performance of Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.
(2) Exclusion Criteria
The literatures addressing any of the exclusion criteria listed below were excluded:
- The literature is not related to Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector.
- The literature doesn’t address the safety and performance of the Digital Flat Panel X-ray
detector.
- The Digital Flat Panel X-ray detector is not the main focus of the literature.
- There is a newer or a more complete study about the same research.
- The full text is not available.
