1. Overview
1.1 Purpose
This is a document that has written warnings, precautions, and notices about software cyber risks.
1.2 Scope
This cybersecurity labeling is limited to the portable X-ray system(HnX-P1, HnX-PB).
The variant model HnX-PB has only changed the exterior design compared to the existing model
HnX-P1, and the interface, exterior material, internal structure, PCB, software, etc. are all the
same.Therefore, no additional cybersecurity risk management is performed due to the addition of
the variant model (HnX-PB).

• A1: Configuration - Receive kV, mAs, APR setting value information.

• A6: Battery charging - Battery charge current/voltage regulation and monitoring.
• A7: Battery monitoring - Battery charging status monitoring
• A8: Error checking - Checking for occurrence of low voltage, overvoltage, low current,
overcurrent, and overheating

HnX_P1 software does not have a specific EOL/EOS due to the nature of the product. The end

updates. If the device remains in service following the end of support, the cybersecurity risks for
end-users can be expected to increase over time.
