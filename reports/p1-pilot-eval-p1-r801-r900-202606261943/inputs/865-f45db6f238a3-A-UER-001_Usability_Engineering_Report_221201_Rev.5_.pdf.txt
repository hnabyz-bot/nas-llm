6.1.1 Intended Purpose
HAD1717MC and HAD1717MG are indicated for digital imaging solution designed for general
radiographic system for human anatomy. It is intended to replace film or screen based radiographic
systems in all general purpose diagnostic procedures. Not to be used for mammography.

Radiographic imaging of human body parts such as head, Chest, spinal, abdominal, particular, hand,
foot and other internal organs.

6.11 Usability Validation
6.11.1 Purpose
This device applies constant heat to the human body and is used to relieve muscle pain.
6.11.2 Test results for each intended user
Five intended users were completed in connection with the usability of the medical warmer (BPD-
1700 G) product. Each intended user satisfied all the performance conditions of the detector
HAD1717MG (HAD17MC) product and, accordingly, all usability objectives (test criteria) were
satisfied. Since the detector HAD1717MG (HAD1717MC) product is not a high-risk product, it is found
that the intended user understands the product well and is easily usable depending on usability.
