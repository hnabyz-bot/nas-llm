1. Purpose
This Document is to have proper managing procedure to prepare
in demands of Language Translation Procedures of Customer.
2. Scope
This Standard applies to HAD1717MG, HAD1717MC User Manual
translation procedure
3. Definition
3.1. Document
Any form of document issued in purpose of Quality Control work
3.2. Language
Any custom system to communicate thoughts and feeling
in verbal or character
4. Responsibility and Authority
4.1. Development in Charge is responsible to translate
User Manual in demands of customer. And could request
outside professional for translation in necessity.
4.2. Development Head Manager has responsible for internal review
and request approval to Quality Control Head Manager.
4.3. Quality Control Head Manager is responsible for final approval,
and distributes data to related department.
4.4. Overseas Sales Department is responsible to inform customers
about translated Manual
5. Work Procedure
Work approval and procedure is as below
