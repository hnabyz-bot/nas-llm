1. Operating Precautions
1) Since TFT sensor is uncovered, please be cautious and do not scratch it.
2) Keep away from water, dust and fire.
3) When the panel surface or backside is stained, clean it with air-gun blowing or soft dust-free wiper
carefully.
4) The substrate of TFT sensor is glass, and may be broken or cracked if dropping or bumping.
5) Remember to avoid ESD (Electro Static Discharge) damage and chemical damage.
6) TFT sensor is not allowed to be twisted or bent. Please be cautious when dealing in further process.
7) In case if a sensor has to be put back into the packing tray after once it was taken out from the
container, press at the far ends of the sensor edge softly. Otherwise the TFT sensor may be
damaged.
8) Keep TFT sensor in dark environment without using or it might have the residual image of light or
cause abnormal image when using.

Note3. Definition of TFT Leakage
The value of TFT leakage is to illustrate the TFT light performance in the sensor array.
• Several dark images are acquired until the pixel counts are stable in the dark environment.
• Set IFS(Integrator full-scale selection) of AD71124 : IFS = 63(decimal value).
• TFT plate is exposed by strong green light with 10sec duration and capture light image as LightImage_10sec.
• Several dark images are acquired until the pixel counts are stable in the dark environment.
• TFT plate is exposed by strong green light with 20sec duration and capture light image as LightImage_20sec.
• Substrate two image using (LightImage_10sec - LightImage_20sec ).
• Calculate Median value of (LightImage_10sec - LightImage_20sec ) as ( CNT10 – CNT20 ).

beyond the pallet.

and band to fix on pallet, also can refer to method(2).
