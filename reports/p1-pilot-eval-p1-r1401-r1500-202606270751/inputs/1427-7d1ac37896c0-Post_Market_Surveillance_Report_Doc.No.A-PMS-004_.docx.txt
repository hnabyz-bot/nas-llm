Intended Use

ADD(Abyz Digital Detector), Digital Flat Panel X-Ray Detector is indicated for digital imaging solution designed for providing general radiographic diagnosis of human anatomy targeting both adult and children. It is intended to replace film based radiographic diagnostic systems and provide more precise case diagnosis and treatment planning on a real time basis for physicians and radiologists. Not to be used for mammography.

Details of Adverse Events from Similar Device

SD-01

EU

MDA/2020/010

Summary

The manufacturer issued a Field Safety Notice, dated 13 February 2020, to inform users of the potential for the end cap component of affected devices to separate from the housing tube after implantation. The end cap is a threaded component which is designed to prevent the ingress and egress of fluid from the mechanism of the MAGEC System. This component may de-thread after implantation, exposing internal components of the actuator. This could lead to accelerated degeneration of the mechanism and release of titanium alloy wear debris. This issue affects only the MAGEC Model X device generation.

The manufacturer says that affected devices might still distract and/or continue serving as an internal brace despite separation of the end cap. However, the aim of this MDA is to provide additional guidance on the clinical follow-up of patients implanted with affected devices

Analysis

This adverse event is not related to the digital flat panel x-ray detector.

SD-02

MDA/2020/020

Summary

The manufacturer issued a Field Safety Notice dated 13 November 2019 to inform users of the potential of intra-operative or post-operative cracking of the USS II Polyaxial 3D Head rings, which may result in loosening of the rods, poor spinal mechanics, non-union or malunion, pain or dislocation.

The manufacturer reports that the majority of implant failures have occurred and been identified during the procedure.

However, there is the potential for cracking to occur and / or be identified post-operatively. Therefore, the aim of this MDA is to provide additional guidance on the clinical follow-up of patients implanted with affected devices.

4. PMS Result

As a result of the post-2020.06 market research analysis, no additional measures such as product design change and process improvement are required. Therefore, follow-up (corrective and preventive measures) to the post-market research conducted this time are not carried out.

In the event of serious adverse events and an accident with our product, Field Safety Corrective Action will be implemented in accordance with MEDDEV 2.12 Post-Market Surveillance. We will issue a notice at the same time to all affected jurisdictions and to those responsible for the H&abyz or representative.

The FSCA notice shall include the following:

- Relevant parts from risk analysis

- Reasons and basic information for the FSCA, including analysis of possible hazards associated with the lack or defect of a device and associated with the continued use of the device

- Explanation and reason of corrective and preventive action

- Advice reported and taken by the user or distributor

- Device/ Serial/ Lot/ Batch number range

- Explain that if the lot and lot areas are taken, the other device will not be affected

- Certification of manufacturer and representative (address, contact)

H&abyz will include a copy of the Field Safety Notice (FSN) Notice submitted to the competent authority. It will proceed before and at the same time as the FSCA is started.

The FSN will be written in a language approved by the competent authority as follows:

a) For emergency FSN, a clear title based on the generic name of the product concerned, the type of FSCA-creation date and measures

b) Possible details of the device type, model name and number of the device ordered, relevant products such as batch/lot/serial number, etc.

c) The truth is that the lack and defect of a device, the definition of possible hazards associated with continued use of the device, the continued use of the device, the definition of possible risks associated with the patient or with the prior use of the device.

d) The advice of action taken by the user includes:

- Device verification and warranty period

- Reuse, disposable method or device changes

The FSCA and FSN form is according to Field safety corrective action – FSCA and Field safety notice template in MEDDEV 2.12 Post Market surveillance.
