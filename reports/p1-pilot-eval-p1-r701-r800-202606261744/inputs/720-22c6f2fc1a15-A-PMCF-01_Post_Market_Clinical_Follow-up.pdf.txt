2. Device description
2.1 Overview
This is a wired digital x-ray flat panel detector, a faster and more efficient digital radiography system. The
detector is used in combination with a TFT glass and scintillator (CSI, GDX) and supports automatic trigger
signal detection technology that can be used without generator integration.
2.2 Device basic information
Product Name Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector
Trade Name
(Model Name) HAD1717MG, HAD1717MC
Class Class Ⅱa
Manufactured
by H&abyz Co., Ltd.
2.3 Intended use
HAD1717MC and HAD1717MG are indicated for digital imaging solution designed for general radiographic
system for human anatomy. It is intended to replace film or screen based radiographic systems in all general
purpose diagnostic procedures. Not to be used for mammography.

3. Post market clinical follow-up plan
The objective of the Post Market Clinical Follow-Up (PMCF) is to confirm clinical performance and
safety throughout the expected lifetime of the medical device, the acceptability of identified risks and
to detect emerging risks on the basis of factual evidence.
The decision to conduct PMCF study was based on the identification of possible residual risks and
unclarity on long term clinical performance that may impact the benefit/risk ratio.

4. Discussion and Conclusion
The PMCF study is not required for digital x-ray flat panel detector, because the safety and clinical
performance are already known from previous use of the device in 20 years. And the appropriate
post-market surveillance can provide sufficient data to address the risks.
According to the above PMCF plan, if additional PMCF research is needed according to
circumstance check, we will proceed the PMCF plan.
