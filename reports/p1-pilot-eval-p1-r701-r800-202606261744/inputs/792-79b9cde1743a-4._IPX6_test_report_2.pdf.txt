1. Introduce
A1717MCW, A1417MCW and F1417MCW have a waterproof function. This test report is about the
waterproof test of X-ray detectors based on IEC60529:1989+A1:1999+A2:2013 (Degrees of protection
provided by enclosures (IPX6)).
.

(1) IP code : IPX6
(2) Delivery rate : 100L/min ± 5%
(3) Displacement : From the nozzle to sample’s surface (2.5~3m)

(7) Check point : After test

3. Test results
(1) Summary
As a result of spraying water using a nozzle, some water penetration was found. However, there was
no water in the main parts for operation. After completing the waterproof test, there was no
abnormality in the operation test.

(3) Operation test

4. Conclusion
The IPX6 test revealed a small amount of water ingress, but no water entered the main
operating parts. Furthermore, the results of the pre-test and post-test tests on the
same specimen confirmed that the product's normal operation was unaffected.
reviewed the test results, we confirmed that it was suitable for IPX6.
