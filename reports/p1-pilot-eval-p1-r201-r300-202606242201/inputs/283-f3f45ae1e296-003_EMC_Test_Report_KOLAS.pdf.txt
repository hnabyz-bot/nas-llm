Description of the ME EQUIPMENT or ME SYSTEM
Additional detail : Describe all devices, racks, modules,
boards, cables, etc. belonging to the ME EQUIPMENT or
ME SYSTEM.
To obtain an X-ray image with any type of X-ray image
detector the part of the patient to be X-rayed is placed
between the X-ray source and the image receptor to
produce a shadow of the internal structure of that
particular part of the body. X-rays are partially attenuated
by dense tissues such as bone, and pass more easily
through soft tissues. Areas where the X-rays strike darken
when developed, causing bones to appear lighter than the
surrounding soft tissue.
The user can acquire images by connecting Switch Box to
X-ray system that is already installed.By pressing the
exposure switch connected to Switch Box, the detector is
activated and ‘Ready’ sign can be seen. When prepared,
the detector delivers exposure signals to X-ray
System.The detector converts absorbed x-ray to digital
data, deliver it to PC via AP (Access Point) or main
cable,and PC converts the delivered digital data into
images to show it on the monitor.
