Product Description
This product is a medical device intended for use by a qualified/trained physician or technician for the purpose
of acquiring X-ray images of the desired parts of patient’s anatomy (including head, cervical spine, chest,
abdomen, lumbar spine, pelvis and extremities).
Characteristics of this product are follows.
1. Type of protection against electric shock: Internally powered and/or Class I (Charger (AC/DC Adaptor))
2. Degree of protection against electric shock: No applied part
3. Classification according to the degree of protection against ingress of water:
1) IPX0: Main unit
4. This equipment is not suitable for use in the presence of flammable anesthetises or oxygen.
5. Mode of operation: Non-continuous operation (5 min (between each X-ray exposure))
