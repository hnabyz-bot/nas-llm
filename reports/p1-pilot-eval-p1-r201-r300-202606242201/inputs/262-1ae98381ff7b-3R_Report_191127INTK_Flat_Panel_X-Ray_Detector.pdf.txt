Section 2: Assessment of recovery rate
The following assessment of the recoverability of materials is based on the utilisation of
generally available recycling and recovery technologies. Not all recycling technologies are
available in all regions. This is a technical recovery assessment. In some EU member states,
certain recovery technologies may be utilised at some times but not at others, or in some
localities but not in others. This often depends not on technical viability but rather on
financial viability based on fluctuating material prices. Other market aspects outside the
control of this report which may impact recycling include transport distances, recyclate
prices, recycling centre staffing costs, state financial incentives, government regulations,
variations in municipality household collection systems, and consumer demand. In addition,
new recycling technologies are constantly being developed, which are likely to lead to
higher recycling rates in future. This report outlines what can be recycled or recovered from
a technical perspective and so made available to the recycling market.
