3. Summary of Hazard
Most important hazard
Health hazard ： No influence on health is anticipated in ordinary use or processing. Environmental
effect ： Littering should be controlled, as wild animals may erroneously eat it to die.
Physical & chemical hazard ： It is combustible and may generate hazardous carbon monoxide or hydrocarbons
by combustion or thermal decomposition.
4. First aid
When inhaled ： If someone inhale crushed powder of the product, take him out to a space of fresh
air and seek for physician’s counsel.
When clings to skin ： If hot molten product clings to skin, treat the burn by cooling the spot with ample
clean cold water and seek for physician’s counsel.
When gets in eye ： Despite the product is made of flexible material, it may hurt eye ball when gets in
eye. Clean the eye with ample clean water and seek for physician’s counsel.
When swallowed ： Try to vomit. If anything strange or abnormal is felt, seek for physician’s counsel.
5. Fire fighting procedure
Extinguishing agent ： Use water, foam agent, powder agent or carbon dioxide

Method of fighting ： Directly eject extinguishing agent to the flaming product. Care should be taken not

6. Actions for leak or scatter
Effect on human body ： There is no bad influence to human body in ordinary use, processing and storage of
the product.
Effect on environment ： Littering should be controlled, as wild animals may erroneously eat it to die. Care
should be taken not to scatter bits of the product.
Removal method ： Carefully collect the scattered pieces and store.
7. Precaution for handling and storing
Handling ： Gently handle the product which is soft.
Storage ： It falls under the category of ‘Designated Combustible Materials’ of Fire Defense
Law and the relevant regulations. For storage or handling of 20 cu. Meters of the
product, a notification to the controlling Fire Department is required.
Do not apply load to the soft product during storage. Store the product in a cool,
well ventilated space, shielding direct sun beam. Packing material does not have
to be particular but weatherable material is preferred.

material of Fire Defense Law
