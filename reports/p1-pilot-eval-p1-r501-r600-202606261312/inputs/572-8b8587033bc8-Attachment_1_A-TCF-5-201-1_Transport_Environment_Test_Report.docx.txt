Introduction

The report is to evaluate the verification of the transport system method of Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector packaged in box. The report provides a method and acceptance criteria of evaluation on the ability of the device packaged in box to withstand the distribution environment by subjecting them to a test plan consisting of a sequence of anticipated hazard elements encountered in an anticipate distribution environment.

Intended Purpose

The A1717MCW/A1417MCW/F1417MCW is indicated for digital imaging solution designed for providing general radiographic diagnosis of human anatomy targeting both adult and children. It is intended to replace film based radiographic diagnostic systems and provide more precise case diagnosis and treatment planning on a real time basis for physicians and radiologists.

Discussion

As the transport environment test progressed, the sample was subjected to vibration and shock tests. No anomalies were found in the shipping box during visual inspection and the performance waws maintained as confirmed by the performance check.

Conclusion

This transport environment test and verification process confirmed that our packaging system can protect the product itself and maintain its performance under normal transportation conditions and concluded that our packaging system and transport process are valid.
