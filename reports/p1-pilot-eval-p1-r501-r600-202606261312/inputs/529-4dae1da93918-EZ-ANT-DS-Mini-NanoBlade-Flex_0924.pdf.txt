Mini NanoBlade Flex
Dual-Band Flexible Planar Antenna
2400-2500 MHz/4900-5875 MHz
The Ezurio Mini NanoBlade Flex antenna features a flexible printed circuit board that supports WLAN
applications. The flexible board can be embedded in space-sensitive applications where a curved housing
does not provide a flat surface for antenna mounting. The antennas are specifically designed to be embedded
inside devices for aesthetically pleasing integration.
1 Features and Benefits
• Dual-band frequency coverage
• RoHS Compliant (2011/65/EU)
• Flexible PCB for mounting in curved housing
