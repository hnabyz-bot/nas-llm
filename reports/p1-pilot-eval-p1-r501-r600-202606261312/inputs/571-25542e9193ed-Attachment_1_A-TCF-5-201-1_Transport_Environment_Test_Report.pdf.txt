1 Introduction
The report is to evaluate the verification of the transport system method of Digital Flat Panel X-
ray Detector packaged in box. The report provides a method and acceptance criteria of
evaluation on the ability of the device packaged in box to withstand the distribution environment
by subjecting them to a test plan consisting of a sequence of anticipated hazard elements
encountered in an anticipate distribution environment.
2 Product Description
2.1 Product Name
Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector
2.2 Model Name
A1717MCW / A1417MCW / F1417MCW
2.3 Intended Purpose
The A1717MCW/A1417MCW/F1417MCW is indicated for digital imaging solution designed for
providing general radiographic diagnosis of human anatomy targeting both adult and children. It
is intended to replace film based radiographic diagnostic systems and provide more precise
case diagnosis and treatment planning on a real time basis for physicians and radiologists.

6 Discussion
As the transport environment test progressed, the sample was subjected to vibration and shock
tests. No anomalies were found in the shipping box during visual inspection and the
performance waws maintained as confirmed by the performance check.
7 Conclusion
This transport environment test and verification process confirmed that our packaging system
can protect the product itself and maintain its performance under normal transportation
conditions and concluded that our packaging system and transport process are valid.
