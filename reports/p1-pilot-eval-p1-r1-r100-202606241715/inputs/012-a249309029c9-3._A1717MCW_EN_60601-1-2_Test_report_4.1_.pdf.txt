placed a non-conducting support 0.1 m above the supporting plane and at least 0.4 m from the reference
ground plane (wall), EUT was placed at least 0.8 m from ISN.
During the test at telecommunication port of LAN, the 3 m category 7 cable between EUT and ISN
was used.
All other test setup was done properly, according to relative standard.
The measurement at telecommunication port was conducted in the frequency range 150 ㎑ to 30 ㎒.
A peak detector scan was conducted over the defined frequency range.
