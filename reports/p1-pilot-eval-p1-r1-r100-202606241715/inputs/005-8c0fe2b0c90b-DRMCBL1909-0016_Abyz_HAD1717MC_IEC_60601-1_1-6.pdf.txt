General product information:
Product Description
- The HAD1717MC and HAD1717MG are a wired digital flat panel detector that has been designed for a
faster, more streamlined approach to digital radiography systems. The HAD1717MG and HAD1717MC
detector utilizes a combination of propriety TFT glass and scintillators(GDX and CsI), and those and
electronics are housed in one package. The detectors support an auto-trigger signal sensing technology
that allows the detectors to be used without generator integration.
Technical Considerations
- Max. Normal operation: Equipment was repeatedly operated with image scan and transmission.
- The degree of protection against harmful ingress of water is Ordinary(IPX0)
- The product is classified as Class I and no applied part.
- The product has been evaluated as a continuous operation and has not been evaluated for use in
the presence of flammable anaesthetic mixture with air, oxygen or nitrous oxide.
- The Switching Power Supply is certified with TUV Rheinland according to the IEC 60601-1:2005+A1 and
EN 60601-1:2006+A11+A1+A12 (Model: RPS-200-12)
- This Digital Flat Panel X-ray Detector consists of a combination of Detector, and associated equipment
such as Power Supply, Trigger cable, Data cable and LAN cable.
- The Detector (HAD1717MC (Alt. HAD1717MG)) power was supplied from Power Supply (HAD1717M).
- Operating environment specification of The HAD1717MC (Alt. HAD1717MG) is following:
● Ambient temperature range: (5 to 40) ℃
● Relative humidity: (30 to 75) % R.H.
● Atmospheric pressure: (700 to 1 060) hPa
- Storage and transport environment specification of The HAD1717MC (Alt. HAD1717MG) is following:
● Ambient temperature range: (-10 to 50) ℃
● Relative humidity: (10 to 80) % R.H.
● Atmospheric pressure: (500 to 1 060) hPa
- The product was not investigated to the following additional standards or clauses:
Clause 11.7. Biocompatibility (ISO 10993-1)
