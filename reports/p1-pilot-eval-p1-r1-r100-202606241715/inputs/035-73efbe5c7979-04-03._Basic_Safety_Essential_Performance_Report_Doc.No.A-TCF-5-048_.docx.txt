2. Basic Safety, Essential Performance and Pass/Fail Criteria as determined by the Manufacturer

Description of BASIC SAFETY and ESSENTIAL PERFORMANCE

BASIC SAFETY and ESSENTIAL PERFORMANCE.

Operation check of the product can be monitored by using the acquisition software.

- Acquisition image is obtained as intended.

- The acquired image should not have two lines adjacent or be blank.

- Operation normally when operation is required.

- DC power is consistently supplied from POWER SUPPLY when EMC test is performed.

- When connected to POWER SUPPLY, program provided by manufacturer is operated normally.
