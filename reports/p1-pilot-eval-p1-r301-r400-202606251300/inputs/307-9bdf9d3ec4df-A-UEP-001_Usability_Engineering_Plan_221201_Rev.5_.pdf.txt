This document is intended to plan the usability of the detector HAD1717MG,HAD1717MC.
2. Normative references
■EN ISO 14971:2019, Medical devices – Application of risk management to medical devices
■ EN 62366-1:2015 - Medical devices - Application of usability engineering to medical devices
3. Terms and definitions
For the purposes of this standard, the terms and definitions given in "Common Criteria for Electrical
and Mechanical Safety of Medical Devices" [Appendix 3] and EN 62366:2008 and the following
definitions apply.
■3.1 OPERATOR-EQUIPMENT INTERFACE
Means of communication between the operator and the ME equipment [ANSI/AAMI HE 74:2011,
amend definition 3.24]
NOTE 1) Annexes are considered to be part of the ME Equipment and Operator-Interface.
■3.2 OPERATOR PROFILE
A summary of the mental, physical and demographic characteristics of the intended group of
operators as well as the special characteristics that can influence design decisions, such as
professional technical and business requirements.

6. USABILITY ENGINEERING PROCESS
6.1 Application specification
6.1.1 Intended Purpose
HAD1717MC and HAD1717MG are indicated for digital imaging solution designed for general
radiographic system for human anatomy. It is intended to replace film or screen based radiographic
systems in all general purpose diagnostic procedures. Not to be used for mammography.
6.1.2 Patient population
Radiographic imaging of human body parts such as head, Chest, spinal, abdominal, particular, hand,
foot and other internal organs.
a) Age: Available all people (Not to be used for infant or pregnant women)
b) Weight: not relevant
c) Height: not relevant
d) Nationality: multiple
e) Patient group/state: No specification
d) Applications parts: Part of body
f) Disease: Radiographic imaging of human body parts such as head, Chest, spinal, abdominal,
particular, hand, foot and other internal organs.
6.1.3Apply
6.1.4.1Application parts: Part of body
6.1.4.2 Intended user
1) Education: A medical professional with a professional license
2) Knowledge: Conditions that satisfy both of the following two conditions
- A medical professional with a professional license
- familiar with user manual
3) Language Understanding: Korean
4) Experience: No specification

10. Training
If training on the specific MEDICAL DEVICE is required for the safe and effective use of a
PRIMARYOPERATING FUNCTION by the intended USER, the MANUFACTURER shall do at least
one of the following:
– provide the materials necessary for training
– ensure that the materials necessary for training are available
– provide the training.
Where training is required, the annex shall describe the available training options and include
suggestions for such training periods and cycles. Where training is required, intended use objectives
and user profiles should be the basis for training and teaching materials.
