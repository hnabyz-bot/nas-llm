Document Overview

This document aims to provide cybersecurity lifecycle risk analysis and threat modeling for Retrofit X-ray System(HnX-R1).

Medical Device Overview

Product name: Retrofit X-ray System

Model name: HnX-R1

Manufacturer: H&abyz Co., Ltd.

This Retrofit radiographic system (Model: HnX-R1, 가칭) consists of several kinds of X-ray Flat Panel Detector, its accessories and acquisition Software/Viewer Console.

This device consists of 2G model series - A1417MCW, A1717MCW,F1417MCW / Blue model series G1417CW, G1417CWP, G1417FW, G1417FWP, G1717CW, G1717CWP, G1717FW, G1717FWP – wired / wireless digital flat panel detector - battery-operated portable detector, designed and manufactured by H&abyz.

Compared with traditional X-ray products, this device has been designed for faster, more streamlined approach to digital radiography systems. Also HnVUE, acquisition SW console, is the name of Digital Radiography Console Software of H&ABYZ Co., Ltd. HnVUE is a DR Console Software that provides image acquisition from Flat Panel Detector, high-speed image processing for image interpretation, and DICOM service for linking with PACS.

The detector consists of TFT and Scintillator (CsI) and an electronic device that converts data read through it into digital signals. The detectors support an auto exposure detection technology that allows the detectors to be used without generator integration. Of course, at the same time, the HnVue Console program provides the ability to interface with the generator via wires.

The flat panel sensors of the of 2G model series - A1417MCW, A1717MCW,F1417MCW / Blue model series G1417CW, G1417CWP, G1417FW, G1417FWP, G1717CW, G1717CWP, G1717FW, G1717FWP are fabricated using thin film technology based on amorphous silicon technology. Electronically, the sensors are much like conventional photodiode arrays. Each pixel in the array consists of a light‐sensing photodiode and a switching Thin Film Transistor (TFT) in the same electronic circuit. The amorphous silicon photodiode is sensitive to visual light, and its response curve has almost the same performance as human vision. The sensitivity of amorphous silicon photodiodes peaks in green wavelengths, well matched to scintillators such as CsI. It also has excellent linearity according to the exposure dose.

HnVUE is a software that is based on FS-MLW (Faster Specialized Multi Layered Wavelet) technology and is linked to the detector or X-Ray Generator of an existing certified X-ray system company. This software is used for devices that store, enlarge, reduce, view, analyze, transmit, process, and output medical images.

The system is for X-ray imaging and diagnosis in facilities with portable or fixing sites.

This HnX-R1 is not intended for mammography.

The device can be integrated with third-party supplied X-ray generators or analog general radiography systems. This portable X-ray System is designed for portable image capturing device. Model HnX-R1(가칭) can be configured with IEC 60601- 1 safety standard.

The cybersecurity risk of the HnX-R1(가칭) have been addressed to assure that no new or increased cybersecurity risks were introduced as a part of device risk analysis. These risks are defined as sequence of events leading to a hazardous situation, and the control for their risks were treated and implemented as proposed in the risk analysis (e.g., requirements, verification)
