1.0 SCOPE
This document provides the product requirement for the X216AJ2-101, 44.0cm x 52.0cm
amorphous silicon (a-Si) sensor array. The process flow of this product is TFT array on PI glass,1st
cut. The Cell is composed of a TFT panel PI glass substrate on which materials are deposited to
fabricate the thin film transistor (TFT), PD sensor layer, bias lines and contact pads and scintillator. ,
those enable the connection of the pixel array to the readout electronics.
2.0 PURPOSE
This document provides the product requirements for the X216AJ2-101, 44.0cm x 52.0cm
amorphous silicon (a-Si) sensor array, cell product.
3.0 PERSONNEL
This document applies to customers with external supplier on the 44.0cm x 52.0cm
product.
4.0 GENERAL SPECIFICATION
The InnoCare 44 cm x 52 cm sensor array is a two-dimensional array with thin-film transistor (TFT)
switches based on amorphous silicon (a-Si) technology. The array has an approximate pixel area of
35 cm x 43 cm. Pixels are addressed through 3040 data lines and 2466 gate lines. The 7,496,640
pixels are arranged in a matrix of 2466 x 3040. Each pixel size is 140 μm x 140 μm.

7.0 Appearance visual check specification
All TFT arrays are visually inspected. The visual inspection includes an inspection of the arrayagainst a white
light (light condition: 600Lux~1000Lux) to identify gross defects such as:
◆ No crack over 0.3mm length and width at glass edge.
◆ The breach angle of glass is over 90 degree and do not damage the mark on glass edge.
◆ No scratches and cracks on active area and bonding pad.
◆ No surface debris and contamination on active area and bonding pad.
◆ No missing material and metal residue on TFT array.

8.3 ELECTROSTATIC DISCHARGE SENSITIVITY
This device is sensitive to electrostatic discharge (ESD). Handling this device requires adoption of
a general ESD control program according to 099-JESD625-A, SCD, Requirements for Handling
Electrostatic-Discharge-Sensitive (ESDS) Devices. The specification target is HBM class 1B (Voltage
range > 500 but <1000V, as described in ANSI/ESD STM5.1-2001).
The fundamentals are as follows:
• ESD protective work surface
• ESD protective floor mats
• Wrist straps
• ESD protective smocks
• Air ionizers
• Removal of static generating sources from ESD protected areas
• ESD protected handling and packaging materials (antistatic-containers)
• ESD protected area identification (warning signs and labels)
• ESD protection level monitoring (surface resistance, static fields, wrist monitors)
• ESD audits
Workstations must also include, or be in, the presence of airflow ionizers, ensuring that arrays
will be in the presence of ionized airflow for a period of one minute before and after
transferring arrays between workstations.
