1. Warranty
1.1 Incoming inspection
The buyer (customer) shall inspect the appearance of Sensor within three months since the delivery date
(the ““inspection period””) at its own cost. The results of the inspection (acceptance or rejection) shall be
recorded in writing, and a copy of this writing will be promptly sent to the seller.
1.2 Product Warranty
If the buyer can’t finish the visual Inspection within three months, the buyer has to announce any change
of incoming inspection plan at least one week prior to the delivery date. Two parties shall have a meeting
to get the consensus on this matter within two weeks. The buyer may, under commercially reasonable
reject procedures, reject an entire lot in the delivery involved. Within the inspection period, if the Sensors
are within a lot show a number of unacceptable defects in accordance with this incoming inspection
standards, the buyer must notify the seller in writing of any such rejection promptly, and not later than
within three business days during in the end of the inspection period.
Should the buyer fail to notify the seller within the inspection period, the buyer’s right to reject the Sensors
shall be lapsed and the Sensors shall be deemed to have been accepted by the buyer.

2. Operating Precautions
2.1 Electrostatic Discharge Control
1) Remember to avoid ESD (Electro Static Discharge) damage and chemical damage.
2) Static electricity will damage Sensors; please do not touch the Sensor without any grounded devices
and always keep static electricity under 1KV when handing or assembling.
3) Make sure of operator wearing wrist strap during any Sensor handling process for ESD protection.
2.2 Storage
1) All Sensors should be well packed and be stored in a proper moisture and temperature environment.
2) Keep away from water, dust and fire.
3) Keep TFT sensor in dark environment for storage or it might have the residual image of light or cause
abnormal images when using.
2.3 Usage
1) Please operate Sensor with the correct test voltage/current/light source in suitable temperature to
avoid any abnormal damage on it. Be sure to turn off the power when connecting or disconnecting
the circuit.
2) Do not apply the invalid signal; otherwise, it will cause improper shut down and damage the Sensor.
3) Sudden temperature changes cause condensation. High temperature and humidity may degrade
Sensors’ performance. Please do not expose the Sensors to the direct powerful light sources, keep
away from water/dust/fire, and store them in dark environment.
4) No strong vibration or shock or any unsuitable external force is allowed in all procedures of buyer’s
sites, because it may cause Sensors broken and count to buyer’s responsibility.
5) When the panel surface or backside is stained, clean it with air-gun blowing or soft dust-free wiper
carefully, please be cautious and do not scratch it.
6) To take out Sensor from the box should be carefully to avoid damaging the circuit on the sensor.
Please avoid pressure adding to the surface of Sensors
7) Operator is forbidden to touch the front side of Sensor. The front film of Sensor scratches easily,
please handle it carefully. Acetic acid or chlorine compounds are not friends with Sensors.
8) In case if a sensor has to be put back into the packing tray after once it was taken out from the
container, press at the far ends of the sensor edge softly. Otherwise, the TFT sensor may be damaged.
9) There is danger of sensor’s fragments when it is broken, please do not touch the fragments by bare
hands.
