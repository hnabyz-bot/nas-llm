Biocompatibility

The A1417MCW/A1717MCW/F1417MCW contains parts which is intended to be contacted with the body of patient.

So, we perform the systemic approach to a biological evaluation of medical devices according to the ISO 10993-1. The A1417MCW/A1717MCW/F1417MCW has direct contact component (Intact skin, under 24 hours). Material of the A1417MCW/A1717MCW/F1417MCW is same as in commercially available device. But it does not have the same chemical composition with the predicate device. Therefore, we select the biological tests according to the ISO 10993-1 Annex A as below:
