Purpose of the Q-Submission

The primary purpose of this Pre-Submission is to obtain formal feedback from the FDA on a newly established Clinical Image Test Plan for the HnX-R1 (K252912). This plan is designed to address the deficiencies identified in the NSE letter dated January 6, 2026, which raised concerns regarding the validity and integrity of performance data.

[Clinical Image Test Plan]

Q2.1) Regarding the Clinical Image Test Plan, we would like to confirm the adequacy of the selected anatomical regions and the included metadata, such as patient demographics and X-ray exposure parameters.

Q2.2) Furthermore, we request feedback on the appropriateness of our methodology—where a qualified radiographer performs the image acquisition and a board-certified radiologist evaluates the image quality—and the validity of the qualifications of these personnel for this performance validation.
