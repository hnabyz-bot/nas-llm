Intended Purpose

The A1717MCW/A1417MCW/F1417MCW is indicated for digital imaging solution designed for providing general radiographic diagnosis of human anatomy targeting both adult and children. It is intended to replace film based radiographic diagnostic systems and provide more precise case diagnosis and treatment planning on a real time basis for physicians and radiologists.

Indications

It is a tool for acquiring images of human anatomy for physicians and radiologists to diagnose and monitor a patient's condition. The general clinical applications include musculoskeletal imaging to diagnose and monitor bone, chest imaging to diagnose and monitor lung problems, and abdominal imaging and monitor to diagnose intestines problems.

Contra-indications

Local regulations should be reviewed before using those who have limitations in X-rays due to health issues. Make sure to check the conditions of X-ray irradiation in advance.

Not to be used for mammography.

Not to be used for infant and pregnant women.

Not to be used for fluoroscopy and angiography.

Intended Users

a) Education: Completed a healthcare professional training

b) Knowledge: Qualified as a radiologist

c) Language understanding: Language displayed on the label or user manual.

d) Experience: Radiologist, medical imaging specialists

e) To allow a disability:

• Mild reading eyesight impairment or corrected eyesight on log MAR 0.2(6/10 or 20/32)

• Equipment along the one touch or grasp the arm / hand

• Average degree of age-related short-term memory damage

• 40% damage due to normal hearing ranging from 500Hz to 2KHz.
