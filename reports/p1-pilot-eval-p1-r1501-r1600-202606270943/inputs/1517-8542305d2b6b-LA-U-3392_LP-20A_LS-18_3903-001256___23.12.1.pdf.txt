a). Appearance:
There shall be no damage on the surface of plug, connector and cord.
b). Continuity of conductor:
Open / Short circuit is not allowed.
c). Dielectric voltage withstand:
No breakdown of specimen with application of AC 1,250V for 1 minute.
d). Insulation resistance:
Put finished cord into 2.5mm dia. Lead beads with application of DC 500V between one of the
conductors and the grounding wire. Its insulation resistance shall be not less than 100 MΩ.
e). Load test:
a. Plug：
With a pull of 30 pounds weight applied to the cord for 1 minute and the leads can not break
or separate from the body.
b. Security of blades:
A 20 pounds weight was gradually applied to each blade for 2 minutes without loosening
(Displacement not more than 3/32 inch for each blade and grounding pin ).
f). Abrupt pull of test:
Cords employing a grounding conductor:
The six samples shall resist physical abuse and acceptably sustain (A test distance of 10 inches).
a. The grounding conductor should not break before either of the line conductors.
b. Without resulting in discontinuity of any conductor after 5 impacts.

c. The test is discontinued after the maximum of 25 impacts has been reached.
The acceptability of the conductor is to be verified by causing it to carry a current of 40A
at 6-12V for 2 minutes.
g). Blade pull test at elevated temperature:
A weight of 4.55KG is to be attached to each of six samples at the individual line blades.
The oven is then to be maintained at 60℃ for 4 hours, after which the weights are to be removed
and the samples taken from the oven and allowed to cool to room-temperature,without displacing
either blade from its original position in the molded body for more than 1/16 inch (1.6mm).
h). Jacket retention test:
i). Aging test:
Keep samples at 100±1℃ circulating oven for 96 hours. No breakdown will be found.
j). Insertion and withdrawal force test:
The construction of appliance couplers shall allow the easy insertion and withdrawal of the
connector.The withdrawal force should be within the range from 2.5~5kg.
k). Smelling check:
The cable should be checked in smelling and maintained in no-smell status.
l). Specification on color:
Delta E shall be less than 2.0.
